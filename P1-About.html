ABOUT

Forgotten Wind

Forgotten Wind is a Dubstep/Riddim and Future Bass - Music Producer. Though he produces Lo-Fi/Chill Hop songs on the side, his main style of composing is heavily inspired by Bass Music. Producers such as Virtual Riot, Borgore, Kaval, Porter Robinson, Eliminate, etc., are his biggest inspiration. His musical career started way back on 2017 where he was first introduced to his very first Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) through his friend.

He instantly fell in love with music and started to explore the World of Music. Since then he has listened to countless of musical pieces and tracks, be it whatever genre, same thing with the songs he has made though unreleased. Forgotten Wind aims to bring his Music and his Sounds throughout the World and thus the creation of this store. A place where he distributes or even sells his works of the highest quality he can produce with the goal of spreading his influence amongst the musical community.
